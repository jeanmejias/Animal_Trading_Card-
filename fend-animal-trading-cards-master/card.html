Blackbird

The male is all black except for a yellow eye-ring and bill and has a rich, melodious song. The adult female and juvenile have mainly dark brown plumage.

  • Scientific Name: Turdus Merula
  • Weight: 100 g
  • Classification: Species
  • State of conservation: Stable

The Blackbird (Turdus merula) is a species of true thrush. It is omnivorous, eating a wide range of insects, earthworms, berries, and fruits.
